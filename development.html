ABOUT OUR GAME

Hunt of The Ore is an action video game that represents a utopian world in which you must risk your life to collect as much Neonite as you can, while fleeing from city authorities. All this while driving a ship at high speeds with which you must dodge or shoot your enemies to escape alive. The Neonite collected will be used to upgrade your ship and save your planet.

Flying over the city, you will not only collect Neonite but there are some orbs that will give you different support. Some of them are:

  • Add 30% health
  • Double the amount of Neonite you have
  • Add extra ammunition to inventory
